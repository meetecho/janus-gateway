Janus WebRTC Gateway: Demo Tests

Plugin demos
Echo Test A simple Echo Test demo, with knobs to control the bitrate.
Streaming A media Streaming demo, with sample live and on-demand streams.
Video Call A Video Call demo, a bit like AppRTC but with media passing through the gateway.
SIP Gateway A rough SIP Gateway demo, allowing you to register at a PBX and call extensions.
Video MCU A Video MCU demo, allowing you to join a video room with up to six users.
Audio Conference An audio mixing/bridge demo, allowing you join an Audio Conference room.
Voice Mail A simple audio recorder demo, returning an .opus file after 10 seconds.
Recorder/Playout A demo to record audio/video messages, and subsequently replay them through WebRTC.
Screen Sharing A webinar-like screen sharing session, based on the Video MCU plugin.

Admin/Monitor A simple page showcasing how you can use the new Janus Admin/Monitor API.
